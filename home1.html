PIRAVOM


Piravom is a municipality in Ernakulam district and a suburb of Kochi in the Indian state of Kerala. It is located with in 31 km southeast of Kochi city center, at the boundary of the Ernakulam and Kottayam districts. Piravom is famous for its Hindu temples and Christian churches. Piravom has a river-front, verdant hills, and paddy fields.

Places to visit

Areeckal waterfalls

Koorumala View Point

Koorumala is second tallest hills in Ernakulam district, Kerala. Kooru is situated in Elanji, Elanji- Edayar road near VISAT College of Engineering. The attraction of this place a wonderful surrounding view from a Approx. 24Ft tall Gallery. Travelers can enjoy heavy wind cool ambiance and easily reach here from surrounding townships Piravom and Koothattukulam. The beautiful Walkway, Rest place and View gallery make this as a sunset point. Visit the destination in Morning or Evening will be more comfortable for travellers.
